Aggregator-safe model

What the platform helps with — and what homeowners verify.

The platform is built to support comparison and request organization. It does not replace direct provider review, quote review, credential checks, or homeowner decision-making.

What we help with

  • Organizing walk-in tub request details before speaking with independent providers.
  • Helping homeowners compare category options such as soaking, safety, hydrotherapy-style, and premium comfort features.
  • Encouraging clearer questions about quote scope, timelines, feature packages, and service area availability.
  • Connecting homeowners with provider options that may fit their request details and ZIP code.
  • Presenting information in a calm, comparison-first format without pressure-heavy contractor language.

What homeowners verify

  • Provider licensing, insurance, local requirements, and business credentials.
  • Written quote details, product specifications, labor scope, and any possible additional costs.
  • Warranty terms for the tub shell, door seal, parts, features, labor, and related work.
  • Appointment timing, provider availability, project timelines, and service coverage by ZIP code.
  • Contract terms, cancellation rules, payment schedule, and final provider fit before hiring.

Simple process

The goal is to make the first step feel organized, not overwhelming. Homeowners remain responsible for reviewing providers and choosing independently.

Step 03

Step 04

Independent choice

Provider fit lens

A calmer way to compare provider conversations.

Instead of treating every walk-in tub request the same, homeowners can look at the details that change the conversation: bathroom layout, comfort priorities, feature expectations, timing, and provider fit.

01 Space reality

Door swing, bathroom width, plumbing access, flooring, and existing layout.

02 Comfort priority

Low-step entry, seating position, soaking depth, jets, and daily bathing needs.

03 Provider proof

Licensing, insurance, written quotes, warranty terms, and local availability.

04 Decision clarity

Homeowners compare provider options independently before choosing who to contact.
